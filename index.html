FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What exactly is the Route Audit & Simulation?+
A one-time €1,500 engagement where we take your real routing data — delivery addresses, time windows, vehicle specs, driver constraints — and run it through our mathematical optimisation engine. Within 5 business days you receive a full report showing exactly what you would save in fuel, time, and delivery performance. The fee is credited in full to your subscription if you proceed.
How long does integration take?+
A full integration typically takes 1–3 months, covering ERP/WMS/SAP/Excel connectivity, data migration, constraint configuration, driver app deployment, staff training, and go-live support. This timeline ensures a clean, fully tested system with minimal disruption to your operations. We have completed this process hundreds of times across Baltic and EU markets.
Does Optimistic work with our existing ERP or WMS?+
Yes. Optimistic has a full REST API and pre-built connectors for SAP, 1C, Microsoft Dynamics, Oracle, and Excel/CSV imports. Integration feasibility is assessed during the Route Audit at no extra cost. We've successfully integrated with proprietary WMS, legacy ERP, and custom-built systems across hundreds of client deployments.
How is pricing structured?+
Pricing is based on fleet size — the number of vehicles using the system. We offer monthly and annual plans, with a meaningful discount for annual commitment. For most clients with 10+ vehicles, the full annual cost is recovered within 3–6 months of savings. We share indicative pricing on a call — we don't publish prices online because the right solution depends on your specific operation.
What languages does the driver app support?+
The driver mobile app is available in Latvian, Lithuanian, Estonian, Russian, and English. It works on Android and iOS, supports full offline operation for poor-coverage areas, and requires no special hardware.
Can Optimistic handle our specific constraints?+
Optimistic handles 40+ constraint types including hard and soft time windows, vehicle weight and volume limits, driver certifications, hazmat routing, multi-depot, cold-chain, backhaul, cross-docking, and more. Unusual requirements are assessed during the Route Audit at no extra cost.
Is my delivery data secure and GDPR-compliant?+
Yes. All data is stored in EU-based data centres and processed in full compliance with GDPR. Optimistic maintains appropriate data processing agreements and can provide full documentation for your compliance team. We never share or sell client data.
